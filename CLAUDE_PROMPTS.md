# 15 Sample Prompts for Claude Analysis

Drop your daily `calls_YYYY-MM-DD.jsonl` file into Claude and use these exact prompts to gain insights:

1. "Provide a high-level executive summary of all call volume and resolutions for today."
2. "Analyze the transcripts and list the top 3 most common reasons customers called today."
3. "Identify any calls where the customer expressed high frustration or dissatisfaction."
4. "Extract all action items or follow-up tasks mentioned by agents in the transcripts."
5. "List all unresolved calls (`resolved`: false) and summarize what is blocking them."
6. "Find any explicit mentions of pricing or billing issues across all phone calls."
7. "Calculate the average sentiment score of today's calls on a scale of 1 to 10."
8. "Identify the busiest hour of the day based on the Madrid ISO timestamps."
9. "Highlight any calls where the agent handled a difficult customer exceptionally well."
10. "Generate a bulleted list of any feature requests or product feedback mentioned."
11. "Are there any recurring technical issues reported by multiple customers today?"
12. "Create a structured table listing Customer Phone, Summary of Issue, and Next Action Item."
13. "Cross-reference the `notes` and `transcript` fields to check for any data discrepancies."
14. "Detect if any customer mentioned switching to a competitor or canceling their service."
15. "Draft an optimized follow-up email template for today's unresolved high-priority clients."
